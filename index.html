Sudoku Solver

by LeonardoGonzalez

To start, use one of these options...

1. Click or touch the Sudoku directly and fill the cells (You can use Tab key to move between cells).

2. Select Random Sudoku for us to load a selected puzzle for you.

3. Load Manually with a string.

4. Load a past Sodoku from your history, in case you want to revisit an old challenge.
